The Sindarin Supply Wordbook

A Neo-Sindarin lexicon containing {{word_count}} entries.

This page is intended to serve as a comprehensive dictionary of words that can be used for Neo-Sindarin compositions.

The dictionary contains not only words attested by Tolkien, but neologisms, coined words and phrases, crafted from Primitive Eldarin, Noldorin, Gnomish, and Quenya, tagged with perceived level of acceptance and source, added comments, etymologies, cognates, phonetic development references, logical structure, and inspiration that led to the creation of the word.

The majority of the neologisms are submitted to the Vinyë Lambegolmor server for scrutiny. But the review process is a bit murky and takes a long time to get the word accepted, so they are graded on a scale from 0 to 5, where 0 is unreviewed, 1 is dubious, 2 is debated, 3 is accepted, 4 is established, and 5 is recommended.

The words in this dictionary follow cross-language wordlists, starting with the Swadesh 100 and Swadeh 207 lists, and the Intercontinental Dictionary Series (IDS). These are meant to cover most of the essential human vocabulary, and I think that the majority of words in these lists need to be represented in this dictionary.

Most neologisms are based on existing etymological concepts. When possible, inspirational real-life equivalents are provided. The same standard serves for the absence of certain neologisms. If I think they are too arbitrary or lack etymological basis, they are not added to the list.

Neologisms may change with time based on feedback. Keep an eye on the words you use.
